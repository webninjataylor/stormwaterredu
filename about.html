About Us

Stormwater Edu is a full service stormwater education service with a core offering of Stormwater SCM Inspection and Maintenance training modeled on the extremely successful North Carolina BMP Inspection and Maintenance Program. N.C.’s I&M program began certifying landscapers, engineers, landscape architects, and local government workers in 2006, and has certified over 2500 people since its inception. The N.C. I&M program has helped start many successful stormwater inspection and/or maintenance businesses across the southeastern United States and has resulted in much improved levels of stormwater infrastructure design, installation, and maintenance in its service area. In addition to offering I&M certification, the North Carolina course serves as an excellent stormwater survey short course for new employees or students interested in a career in stormwater. For more information on North Carolina’s program see:
http://www.bae.ncsu.edu/topic/bmp-im/.

Instructors

Instruction is provided by university level stormwater professionals with combined experience of 40+ years in stormwater research, field work, and teaching. Courses are taught by a PhD professional engineer and a professional horticulturalist/entomologist who can offer professional instruction on all aspects of Green Infrastructure and Low Impact Development. Courses can be customized for local or regional needs, emphasizing local regulations, climate, vegetation, and supporting infrastructure. The instructors have given customized courses all over the continental United States and in several foreign countries.

Why is Stormwater BMP Inspection and Maintenance Needed?

Communities across the United States must manage rainfall that runs off roads, streets and parking lots. This runoff is called stormwater. To manage stormwater, many treatment devices, called SCMs, have been built. These devices include: wet retention ponds, dry ponds, bioretention areas, swales, stormwater wetlands, permeable pavement, rainwater harvesting systems.

Opportunities in Stormwater

SCM Inspection and Maintenance offers a business opportunity for inspection by licensed professionals such as engineers and landscape architects, and maintenance by landscape and other green industry professionals. Many local governments perform SCM I&M as well and benefit from professional training. Designers will learn to design SCMs/BMPs with maintenance in mind; landscapers can diversify their business to ensure year-round work in a recession-proof area of work; and suppliers of materials and technology to the stormwater industry can learn about new and existing opportunities to support the stormwater industry.

Continuing Education Credits

SCM I&M short courses are excellent venues for offering professional development credits in engineering, landscape architecture, soil science, and commercial pesticide licensing.
