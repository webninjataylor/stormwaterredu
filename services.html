Services

Full Service

We provide registration processing, payment receipt, course notebooks and other handouts (printed or digital), instruction, professional development crediting, break service, meal catering, testing services, and attendance certification.

Suggested class size: Minimum 25 participants, suggested maximum 50 (to facilitate instructor/class interaction), though larger class sizes can be accommodated.

Suggested fee schedule: $300 per person for full day or day and a half course

Continuing Education: SCM I&M short courses are excellent venues for offering professional development credits in engineering, landscape architecture, soil science, and commercial pesticide licensing.
